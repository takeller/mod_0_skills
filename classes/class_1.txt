Staff (class)
  Attributes:
  - name (string)
  - mailing_address (string)
  - is_clocked_in (boolean)
  - hourly_wage (float)

  Methods:
  - clock_in (clocks the staff member into work modifying the is_clocked_in attribute to true)
  - clock_out (clocks the staff member out of work modifying the is_clocked_in attribute to false)
  - update_mailing_address (changes the staff member's mailing_address attribute to reflect a move of residence)
  - give_10_percent_raise (increases the staff member's hourly_wage attribute by 10%)
