Drink (class)
  Attributes:
  - name (string)
  - price (float)
  - ingredients (array of strings)
  - is_alcoholic (boolean)

  Methods:
  - change_name (changes the name of the drink modifying the name attribute)
  - change_price (changes the price of the drink modifying the price attribute)
  - list_ingredients (lists the ingredients used in this drink using the ingredients attribute)
  - make_virgin (takes any alcohol out of a drink changing the is_alcoholic attribute to false,
    and removing any alcoholic ingredients from the ingredients attribute)
